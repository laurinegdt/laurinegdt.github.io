PROJECT

MIROR MIROR

skills : interaction system development, electronic, 3D, coding

tools : Arduino, VScode, blender

What you see is what you’re taught to want.

An interactive mirror embedded with beauty products that selects items for the user based on their reflection and presence. Framed as a personalized beauty assistant, it simulates convenience and self-care while subtly exposing the mechanisms of automated desire and targeted consumption.

Collaborative project with industrial designers : Mélissa Da Silva Nicolet, Dona Levy, Valentin Meghit
