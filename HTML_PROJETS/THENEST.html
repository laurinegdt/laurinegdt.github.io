PROJECT

THE NEST

skills : game development, vr, 3D, character design, sound design

tools : unreal engine, blender, Nomad Sculpt

To touch is to disturb what sleeps.

The Nest is a VR experience that immerses the viewer inside the nest of an unknown creature. Scattered throughout the space are mysterious eggs containing dormant forms. By physically touching elements in real space, hidden structures inside the headset are activated, unfolding through light and sound. Each interaction slowly reveals what lies within, blurring the boundary between the physical gesture and the virtual response.

Collaborative project with Media and Interaction designer : Dalia Spichtig
