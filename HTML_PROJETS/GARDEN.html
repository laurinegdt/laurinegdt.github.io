PROJECT

GARDENISLAND

skills : 3D, virtual reality, video editing

tools : blender, after effect, metaquest

A world that listens, shifts, and never returns the same.

GardenIsland is a fully 3D-produced immersive film designed for virtual reality. Developed in collaboration with Erased Tapes Records, it explores the relationship between sound, space, and perception through a continuous 360° environment. At a certain point, the experience shifts and perception is disrupted. When it returns, the garden is no longer the same, as if the space itself had been changed through experience.

Collaborative project with Media and Interaction designer : Dalia Spichtig
