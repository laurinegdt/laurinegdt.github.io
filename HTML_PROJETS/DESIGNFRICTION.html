DESIGN FRICTION

skills : interaction system development, electronic, 3D, coding

tools : Arduino, blender

These flowers no longer wait for the sun.

They open at the mere passage of a card, petals trembling, animated by circuits and urgency.
A simple gesture, and yet it reveals a world where nature bends under the weight of our impatience, where beauty no longer blooms: it is commanded.
In the silence of their artificial dance, a question lingers: What have we lost in wanting everything, instantly?

Collaborative project with industrial designers : Léa Perrelet, Arthur Németh
